How to Play ATC Simulator

Objective

Manage air traffic at a busy airport. Guide aircraft safely to land, takeoff, and taxi while handling emergencies and maintaining runway safety.

Getting Started

  • Click on an aircraft in the flight list to select it.
  • View aircraft details and available commands in the control panel.
  • Use the map to visualize aircraft positions and runways.

Commands

  • Land: Select a runway to clear the aircraft for landing. Aircraft will approach from the correct direction.
  • Takeoff: Clear aircraft for takeoff from a selected runway.
  • Taxi: Direct aircraft to taxi to a waiting point for takeoff preparation.

Map Controls

  • Zoom: Use zoom in/out buttons to adjust map scale.
  • Pan: Click and drag on the map to move the view.

Emergencies

Aircraft may experience emergencies (fire, engine failure, etc.). These are indicated by red borders. Handle them carefully

Tips

  • Monitor runway usage - runways are blocked for several minutes after use.
  • Watch for windshear during landings.
  • Keep aircraft separated to avoid conflicts.
  • Act quickly on emergencies to prevent diversions.
